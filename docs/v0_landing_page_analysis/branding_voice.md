# Branding Voice Guide: The "We Got This" Persona (Israel)

This guide defines the verbal identity for "Acme Inc" when targeting the Israeli market. It shifts from generic corporate language to a persona that is assertive, reliable, and locally resonant.

## 1. The Core Persona: "The Fixer" (הביצועיסט)
In Israel, "We got this" isn't just a promise; it's a personality. The brand should sound like a trusted friend who knows exactly how to navigate the system and get things done.

- **Traits**: Assertive, Direct, Warm but Professional, Efficient.
- **The Vibe**: "Don't worry about it, it's already handled." (אל תדאגו, זה עלינו).

## 2. Voice Pillars

### Pillar 1: "Dugri" (Directness)
Israelis value time and honesty. Avoid marketing "fluff."
- **Instead of**: "Our innovative platform leverages cutting-edge technology to facilitate global connections."
- **Use**: "אנחנו מחברים אתכם לכל מקום, בלי סיבוכים." (We connect you anywhere, without complications.)

### Pillar 2: "Ahrayut" (Accountability)
The "We got this" guys take full responsibility.
- **Instead of**: "Support is available for your convenience."
- **Use**: "אנחנו כאן לכל שאלה, תמיד." (We're here for every question, always.)

### Pillar 3: "Zrizut" (Agility)
The voice should feel fast and energetic.
- **Instead of**: "Begin your journey with us today."
- **Use**: "יאללה, בואו נתחיל." (Come on, let's get started.)

## 3. Copy Adaptation (English vs. Hebrew "We Got This")

| Section | Current English (Generic) | Israeli "We Got This" (Hebrew) | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hero Headline** | "We connect you" | **"סוגרים לכם פינה בכל יעד"** | "Closing a corner" is the ultimate Israeli idiom for "we got this/it's handled." |
| **Hero Sub-headline** | "Bridging distances, building relationships" | **"הדרך הכי קלה ומהירה למצוא את המקום הבא שלכם"** | Focuses on speed and ease—what the user actually wants. |
| **CTA Button** | "Get Started" | **"אני בפנים"** | "I'm in"—informal, high energy, and decisive. |
| **Features Header** | "Our Features" | **"מה יוצא לכם מזה?"** | "What's in it for you?"—direct and benefit-oriented. |
| **Contact Section** | "Contact Us" | **"דברו איתנו בוואטסאפ"** | "Talk to us on WhatsApp"—immediate and personal. |

## 4. Tone Do's and Don'ts

| Do | Don't |
| :--- | :--- |
| Use first-person plural ("We/Us"). | Use passive voice or overly formal third-person. |
| Use local slang sparingly (e.g., "תכל'ס", "סגור"). | Use literal translations of American idioms. |
| Be confident and assertive. | Be apologetic or vague. |
| Focus on the solution/result. | Focus on the process/technology. |
