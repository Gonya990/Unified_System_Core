## Keyword Matching Tips

### Do:
- Use exact phrases from job descriptions
- Include both acronyms and full terms (e.g., "SEO" and "Search Engine Optimization")
- Place keywords naturally within achievement bullets
- Include keywords in Skills section AND in context in Experience

### Don't:
- Keyword stuff (using keywords without context)
- Use invisible/white text keywords
- Include skills you can't demonstrate in an interview
- Force keywords that don't fit your experience
