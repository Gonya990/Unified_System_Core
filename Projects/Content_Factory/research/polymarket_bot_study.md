**Topic**: "Trading bot turns $313 into $438,000 on Polymarket in a month"

### The Strategy

* **Arbitrage/Latency Trading**: The bot (Profile: `0x8dxd`) exploited a time lag between real-world price movements (CEXs like Binance/Coinbase) and the odds update on Polymarket.
